Jack's Cellular Automata Explorer

View on GitHub

Cellular automata are discrete models studied in computer science, mathematics, and theoretical biology. This explorer allows you to visualize and experiment with different cellular automata rule sets.

Clicking on a cell toggles its state between alive and dead.

Define the rules for cell birth and survival. For example, Conway's Game of Life is B3/S23 (Birth on 3 neighbors, Survival on 2 or 3 neighbors).
